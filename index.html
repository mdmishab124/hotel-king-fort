About our Hotel

Welcome to "Hotel King Fort", an exclusive economy-class business hotel offering affordability without compromising on quality. Immerse yourself in the essence of "God's Own Country," Kerala, as we invite you to indulge in our range of packages designed for leisure and pleasure.

Located on Kallai Road in Calicut, opposite KOTTAKKAL ARYA VAIDYASALA, "King Fort" has earned a reputation for excellence, providing guests with a blend of quality and value. Our hotel offers ample space and tranquility, boasting a prime vintage location with the railway station and bus station conveniently nearby.

Choose from our well-appointed AC and non-AC rooms, including Standard, Deluxe, Superior Suite, and King Suite, all equipped with modern amenities such as LCD TVs, mini-refrigerators, internet access, 24-hour room service, laundry facilities, and hot and cold water in the bathrooms. Additional conveniences include travel assistance, direct dialing from the room, safe deposit lockers, doctor-on-call services, and acceptance of major credit cards.

Renowned for our pleasing ambiance and delectable cuisine ranging from Indian to continental, Hotel King Fort maintains international standards of hygiene. Our esteemed guests depart with hearts full and cherished memories, reflecting the exceptional experiences they've had during their stay at Hotel King Fort.
